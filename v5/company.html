Company

We are teaching AI what humans love.

Tastry is an interdisciplinary team of chemists, machine-learning researchers, and consumer scientists. We are building the infrastructure layer for predictive product development. One category at a time, starting with the chemistry that nobody else has captured.

Mission

We are teaching AI what humans love, so the world can make products that are tastier, healthier, and built for the people who want them.

For a century the consumer industry has launched products and waited for the market to tell it whether the launch was right. Tastry answers that question before the launch, from the molecule up. Product development becomes predictive, not reactive, so brands can reformulate out the sugar and the additives nobody wants, and put the products people will actually love on the shelf.

The opportunity

Beverage is the wedge. The rest of CPG is next.

Beauty, personal care, fragrance, nutraceuticals, and every other product on the shelf follow. Tastry sits where consumer packaged goods, predictive AI, and analytical chemistry meet, and the category we are ultimately building into is every CPG product made.
