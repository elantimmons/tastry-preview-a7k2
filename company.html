Tastry is an interdisciplinary team of chemists, machine-learning researchers, and consumer scientists. We are building the infrastructure layer for predictive product development. One category at a time, starting with the chemistry that nobody else has captured.

Mission

Product development should be predictive, not reactive.

For a century the consumer industry has launched products and waited for the market to tell it whether the launch was right. Tastry is the model that answers the question before the launch, from the molecule up, so brands can stop guessing what people want, and start knowing.
